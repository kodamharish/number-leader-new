Breakdown TTM {{balance_sheet.date|date:'d-m-Y'}} Definition
Total Assets The sum of all assets owned by a company, both current and non-current.
Current Assets Assets expected to be converted into cash or used up within one year.
Cash, Cash Equivalents & Short Term Investments Liquid assets that can be easily converted to cash within three months.
Cash And Cash Equivalents Actual currency and highly liquid investments readily convertible to cash.
Cash Currency and coins held by the company.
Cash Equivalents Short-term, highly liquid investments that can be quickly converted to cash.
Other Short Term Investments Investments expected to be sold or converted into cash within a year.
Receivables Money owed to a company by its customers or other entities.
Accounts Receivable Amounts customers owe for credit sales.
Gross Accounts Receivable The total amount owed by customers before deducting allowances.
Allowance For Doubtful Accounts Receivable A reserve for estimated uncollectible accounts.
Other Receivables Receivables other than accounts receivable, such as interest or tax refunds.
Inventory Goods available for sale and raw materials used to produce them.
Raw Materials Basic materials used in the production process.
Work in Process Goods that are in the process of being manufactured but are not yet complete.
Finished Goods Completed products ready for sale.
Hedging Assets Current Assets used to offset potential losses from other investments.
Other Current Assets Miscellaneous assets expected to be converted to cash within a year.
Total non-current assets Long-term investments not expected to be liquidated within a year.
Net PPE Property, Plant, and Equipment minus accumulated depreciation.
Gross PPE The total cost of property, plant, and equipment before depreciation.
Land And Improvements Land And Improvements: Land owned by the company and enhancements made to it.
Buildings And Improvements Structures and modifications that increase their value.
Machinery Furniture Equipment Capital assets used in the production process and office equipment.
Other Properties Additional properties owned by the company not classified under other categories.
Leases Long-term rental agreements for property or equipment.
Accumulated Depreciation The total depreciation expense that has been recorded against an asset.
Goodwill And Other Intangible Assets Non-physical assets with long-term value, like trademarks and patents.
Goodwill The excess value paid over the fair market value of assets during an acquisition.
Other Intangible Assets Non-physical assets excluding goodwill, such as patents and copyrights.
Investments And Advances Investments And Advances: Long-term financial investments and prepayments.
Long Term Equity Investment Investments in other companies' equity meant to be held for the long term.
Other Non Current Assets Miscellaneous long-term assets not classified elsewhere.
Total Liabilities Net Minority Interest All obligations the company owes, excluding minority interest.
Current Liabilities Obligations due within one year.
Payables And Accrued Expenses Amounts the company owes to suppliers and expenses incurred but not yet paid.
Accounts Payable Money owed to suppliers for purchases made on credit.
Income Tax Payable Taxes owed to the government that have not yet been paid.
Pension & Other Post Retirement Benefit Plans Current Current portion of liabilities for employee benefits.
Current Debt And Capital Lease Obligation Debt and lease payments due within one year.
Current Debt Short-term borrowing due within a year.
Capital Lease Obligation Current portion of a long-term lease agreement.
Current Deferred Liabilities Liabilities that have been incurred but not yet recognized as expenses.
Current Deferred Revenue Money received for goods or services not yet delivered.
Other Current Liabilities Miscellaneous short-term liabilities.
Total Non Current Liabilities Net Minority Interest Long-term obligations excluding minority interest.
Long Term Debt And Capital Lease Obligation Debt and lease payments due beyond one year.
Long Term Debt Loans and financial obligations not due within the next year.
Long Term Capital Lease Obligation Long-term portion of lease agreements.
Non Current Deferred Liabilities Long-term liabilities that have been incurred but not yet recognized.
Non Current Deferred Taxes Liabilities Taxes accrued but not due within the next year.
Non Current Deferred Revenue Revenue received for goods or services to be delivered beyond one year.
Trade and Other Payables Non Current Long-term payables related to business operations.
Other Non Current Liabilities Miscellaneous long-term liabilities.
Total Equity Gross Minority Interest Total equity, including the ownership interest of minority shareholders.
Stockholders' Equity Owners' claim on the company's assets after liabilities are deducted.
Capital Stock The amount of money invested by shareholders.
Common Stock Shares entitling their holder to dividends and voting rights.
Retained Earnings Profits reinvested in the business instead of being paid out as dividends.
Gains/ (Losses) Not Affecting Retained Earnings Changes in equity not resulting from net income.
Other Equity Adjustments Miscellaneous adjustments to equity not captured in other categories.
